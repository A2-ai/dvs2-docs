**dvs** is built and maintained by [**A2-AI**](https://a2-ai.com).

A2-AI is a scientific consulting firm specializing in pharmacometrics, clinical
pharmacology, and regulatory strategy for drug development. Working as an
extension of their clients' teams, they pair rigorous science with custom
technology and GxP-compliant cloud computing to help pharmaceutical and biotech
organizations accelerate drug approvals. Their mission: **scale science with
confidence**.

dvs grew out of that work. Drug-development programs generate large, sensitive
datasets that have to be versioned, shared, and reproduced exactly — but don't
belong in a Git history. dvs is the independent data version control system
A2-AI built to handle them, usable with Git or entirely on its own.

- Source: [github.com/A2-ai/dvs2](https://github.com/A2-ai/dvs2)
- Based in Ann Arbor, Michigan
